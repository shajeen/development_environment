## Overview

DevEnvOps is a powerful tool designed to streamline the management of your development environments. Whether you're working with Docker containers or Vagrant virtual machines, DevEnvOps provides a unified and intuitive interface to orchestrate your workspaces.

## Key Features

*   **Unified Environment Management:** Seamlessly control both Docker and Vagrant environments from a single application.
*   **Pre-configured Templates:** Utilize a variety of pre-built templates for C++, CUDA, and Python development, accelerating your setup process.
*   **Intuitive GUI:** A user-friendly graphical interface simplifies complex operations, making environment management accessible to all skill levels.
*   **Environment Cloning & Customization:** Easily duplicate existing environments and tailor their configurations to meet specific project requirements.
*   **Real-time Status Monitoring:** Keep track of your development environments with up-to-date status information.
